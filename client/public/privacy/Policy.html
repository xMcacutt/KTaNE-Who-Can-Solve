On the Subject of Privacy

The boring legal stuff

Introduction

KTaNE - Who Can Solve? is a community-driven project designed to store and display player confidence scores for each module in modded Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes. This website uses Discord OAuth for user authentication and stores limited, non-sensitive identifying information in a secure database. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that information.

Data Collection

When you sign in using Discord OAuth, your Discord username, user ID, and avatar hash are collected and stored. This data is used solely to identify you within the site, to display your name and avatar on leaderboards and your account page, and to generate in-game profile names containing your username and ID. This identifying information is not used for any other purpose and is not shared with any third parties.

When interacting with the site, your module confidence scores, bomb favourites, and solo statuses may be collected and stored. This data is displayed publicly through leaderboards and may be accessed via the in-game companion mod or route. It may also be used to generate anonymised statistics or insights to improve the site. This information will never be sold, licensed, or shared outside the scope of the site.

Lawful Basis for Processing

The lawful basis for collecting and processing your information is consent, which you provide by signing into the site using Discord OAuth. You may withdraw your consent at any time by deleting your account and associated data as described below.

Data Storage and Retention

All data is stored securely and retained indefinitely unless deleted by the user or site owner. Users may delete their data at any time by navigating to their account and selecting the “Delete Data” option. This action immediately removes all associated information from the database.

Data Protection Rights (GDPR)

This site operates in accordance with the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. As a data subject, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data at any time.
  • Withdraw your consent to data processing.

You can exercise most of these rights directly through your account page.

Cookies and Local Storage

This site does not use cookies for advertising, analytics, or tracking purposes. Instead, your browser’s local storage is used to maintain information necessary for the site’s functionality, including your session ID, authenticated user data, and temporary records of other users you have added. This information remains on your device, is not transmitted to third parties, and can be removed at any time by logging out or clearing your browser’s local storage for this site.

Security

The site is hosted on a secure server with a valid SSL/TLS certificate. All communications occur over encrypted HTTPS connections. Database access is restricted, and sensitive operations are limited to authenticated users.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements or legal requirements. Any changes will take effect upon publication on this page.
